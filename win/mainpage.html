Why do blockchains and the real world need oracle?

Blockchains are excellent storage systems, where data are tamper-proofing, encrypted and verifiable. However, blockchains cannot access external information, nor decide whether the information is authentic and credible. WinkWin solves the problem of connecting real world data through smart contracts, and ensures the reliability of data through a decentralized mechanism.

What WinkWin does?

Decentralized financial apps provide prices for token exchange and loan mortgage. WinkWin’s embedded price oracle system offers stable and reliable price feeds for DeFi-building developers.

WinkWin Oracle allows developers to use any real-world data, such as sports and weather. Developers could create custom-made oracles here, and connect smart contracts with the real world in a few minutes.

WinkWin's smart contract can easily access any data, forming a trusted data ecosystem in various industries. With faster, stabler and cheaper transactions, it provides better services for developers and users.
